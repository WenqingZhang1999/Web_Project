1.Location Tips:
Use the location tips for the people who has special need in cognition
and age-related aspect helping her or him to keep focus and figure out where her or him is now
( or enhance their sense of orientation ).


2.Color Contrast:
Use strong contrast between the background and content of each page for those
who have special need in vision helping them distinguish the content.


3.Text Size :
Make text in larger size as possible as I can which will work in those
who have special needs in vision and age-related aspect assisting them read more easily.
